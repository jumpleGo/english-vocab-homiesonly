## 📚 Quick Reference by Level

### 🟢 A2 (Start here)
- Basic phrasal verbs: show up, hang out, screw up, burn out, mess up
- Basic reactions: beats me, I'm down, no biggie, my bad, you do you
- Grammar: few/a few, me neither vs me too, arrive in vs at

### 🔵 B1–B2 (Main level for this channel)
- Office phrasal verbs: wrap up, iron out, fall behind, come up with, turn down
- Conversational: keep your cool, get the hang of it, there's a lot to unpack
- Grammar: be supposed to, need to vs needn't, verb patterns (-ing vs to)
- Confusable: affect/effect, economic/economical, beside/besides

### 🟣 C1–C2 (Advanced)
- Advanced phrasal verbs: boil down to, fawn over, bottle up, gloss over
- C1 phrases: I see where you're coming from / not a hill I'm willing to die on
- Grammar: inversions, I wish + Past Perfect, It's high time + Past Simple
- Beautiful words: serendipitous, evanescent, halcyon, ephemeral

## 🎯 How to Use This Repo

1. **Daily practice:** Open any `.md` file, pick 5 items, make your own sentences
2. **Flashcard apps:** Import `vocabulary.json` into Anki or Quizlet
3. **With AI teacher:** Paste items into Claude/ChatGPT and ask for dialogue practice
4. **Level up:** Start A2 → work through B1 → reach C1
