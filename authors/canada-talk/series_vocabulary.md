### Grammar bonus — 3 Conditionals (heard in Ep. 5-6):

| Type | Structure | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **1st conditional** (real future) | If + Present, will + V | If I go, will you come with me? |
| **2nd conditional** (unreal present) | If + Past, would + V | What would you be doing if I wasn't here? |
| **3rd conditional** (unreal past) | If + Past Perfect, would have + V3 | If you had told me, I'd never have believed you. |

> ⚠️ In spoken AmE you'll often hear "If you would have told me…" — this is technically wrong but very common in casual speech.

## 🎶 Intonation Note (Episode 1)

**Yes/No questions** → голос идёт ВВЕРХ ↗ в конце:
- Do you *mind?* ↗
- Could you pretty please help me? ↗

Ровная или падающая интонация делает вопрос грубым или раздражённым.
