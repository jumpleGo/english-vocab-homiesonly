## Paired Set Expressions (binomials)

| Expression | Meaning (RU) | Example |
|---|---|---|
| ins and outs | от и до, все тонкости | You will know all their ins and outs. |
| dos and don'ts | что можно и что нельзя | She gave us the dos and don'ts for the trip. |
| by and large | в общем и целом | By and large, people prefer summer to winter. |
| rights and wrongs | правые и виноватые; добро и зло | The judge considered the rights and wrongs of the case. |
| black and white | однозначный, чёткое деление | The rules are in black and white. |
| pros and cons | за и против | Bear in mind all the pros and cons. |

## Idiom of the Day

### To buy a pig in a poke
**Meaning:** покупать что-то не глядя, не проверив качество → русский аналог «купить кота в мешке»  
**Example:** *I bought this phone online without reading reviews — I really bought a pig in a poke.*  
**Note:** Зафиксирована с 1555 года (Merriam-Webster): "something offered in such a way as to obscure its real nature or worth".
