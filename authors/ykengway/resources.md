## 📋 Author's Vocabulary-Learning Method

1. **Learn phrases, not single words** — note collocations (e.g. "depend on someone", not just "depend")
2. **Spaced repetition schedule:** 15 min → 1 hour → 1 day → 2 days → 1 week → 1 month (use Quizlet)
3. **Engage all senses:** say words aloud, write by hand, act out gestures (especially helpful for verbs)
