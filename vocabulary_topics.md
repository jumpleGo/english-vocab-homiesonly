## 🧠 WORD FAMILY: SPRING (by level)

### B1:
```
spring = пружина       → The mattress has lost its springs.
to spring = прыгнуть   → The cat sprang onto the table.
```

### B2:
```
spring to life         → The city springs to life in the morning.
spring out of bed      → He sprang out of bed when he heard the alarm.
spring up              → New cafés keep springing up in this area.
```

### C1:
```
spring into action     → The team sprang into action immediately.
spring a surprise      → They sprang a surprise party for her birthday.
no spring chicken      → He's no spring chicken, but still full of energy.
```

### C2:
```
spring to someone's defence  → Her colleagues sprang to her defence.
spring a leak                → The boat sprang a leak and started to sink.
spring from something        → His idea sprang from a childhood memory.
```
